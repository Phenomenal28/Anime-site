Naruto
Hero of the hidden leaf

Naruto Uzumaki, a Hyperactive and knuckle-headed ninja, lives in Konohagakure, the Hidden Leaf village. Moments prior his birth, a huge demon known as the Kyuubi, the Nine-Tailed fox, attacked Konohagakure and wreacked havoc. In order to put an end to the Kyuubi's rampage, the leader of the village, the 4th Hokage, sacrificed his life and sealed the monstrous beast inside the newborn Naruto Shunned because of the presence of the Kyuubi inside of him, Naruto struggles to find his place in the village. He strives to become the Hokage of Konohagakure, and he meets many friends and foes along the way

Black Clover
"Push Past Your Limits!"

In a world where magic is everything, Asta and Yuno are both found abandoned at a church on the same day. While Yuno is gifted with exeptional magic powers, Asta is the only one in this world without any. At the age of 15, both recieved grimoires, magic books that amplify their holder's magic. Asta's is a rare Grimoire of Anti-Magic that negates and repels his opponent's spells. Being opposite but good rivals, Yino and Asta are ready for the hardest of challenges to achieve their common dream: to be the Wizard King. Giving up is never an option!

K-Project
"The Kings"

K Project is an action-packed anime series that explores a hidden world ruled by seven kings, each with their own mystical powers and loyal clans. The story follows Yashiro Isana, a high school student falsely accused of murder, as he navigates this complex world of power struggles and supernatural abilities. With stylish animation, intense battles, and deep character dynamics, K Project delivers a gripping tale of mystery and intrigue set in a modern cityscape.

Demon Slayer.
"Set your heart ablaze"

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose world is shattered when his family is slaughtered by demons, leaving only his sister, Nezuko, alive—though transformed into a demon herself. Determined to avenge his family and find a cure for Nezuko, Tanjiro embarks on a dangerous journey, joining the Demon Slayer Corps. Armed with extraordinary swordsmanship and fiery determination, Tanjiro faces off against terrifying demons, each more dangerous than the last. With stunning visuals, heart-pounding action, and emotional depth, Demon Slayer weaves a tale of family, sacrifice, and survival that will leave you breathless and craving more.

Jujutsu Kaisen
Unleash your curse energy

Jujutsu Kaisen centers on Yuji Itadori, a high schooler with extraordinary physical abilities who stumbles upon a cursed object—a rotting finger—that draws him into the dangerous world of curses and sorcery. After swallowing the finger to protect his friends, Yuji becomes the host of a powerful and malevolent curse named Sukuna. To survive, he joins the Jujutsu Sorcerers, elite warriors who battle monstrous curses plaguing the world. With high-stakes battles, dark mysteries, and a thrilling blend of supernatural powers, Jujutsu Kaisen delivers adrenaline-fueled action and intense character development that keeps you on the edge of your seat.

One Piece
The Great Pirate Era

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a spirited young pirate with a dream as vast as the ocean itself—to find the legendary treasure known as "One Piece" and become the Pirate King.
